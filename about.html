About Skintastik

Skintastik Day Spa was built around respect for our clients' time, their demand for value and their need for relaxation and well being. Skintastik was founded by Alyssa Miller, because she experienced first hand the lack of day spas in the Sacramento area that addressed the specific needs and time constraints of their clients. Tired of the fads and cleverly named treatments that provided intangible results, she is focused on providing the best and most reliable services to her clients. Our day spa is located on 2545 E Bidwell Street in Suite 155, right across the street from Folsom Lake College.
